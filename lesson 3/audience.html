Target audience

The target audience of the Edo-best website is described below:

  • Who: The website would target business owners and volunteer residents within the edo region. Businesses outside the region, charity organizations, tourists etc. in need of professionals for their projects could visit the site to make their requests.
    Businesses within the region could also link with each other for a project through the platform
  • Age: There are no age limits to visitors of the website but to be a member, you must have a business that offers professional services.
    The platform offers chances to residents to participate in projects for business that would allow such. The resident would send a message through the "contact us" part of the website if he/she wishes to be part of present or future voluntary projects.
  • Motivation: Raises the standard of living for individuals and foster business growth in the area, promotes communication between local business on how to achieve best results for any project, help residents, tourist and investors navigate through different businesses in the area. etc.
