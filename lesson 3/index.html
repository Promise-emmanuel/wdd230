Site Purpose

The purpose of the Edo-Best website is to connect professionals or businesses within the Edo region with businessmen, charity organizations,individuals etc. that are looking to carry out paid or volunteer projects within the region. This organization is commited to bringing together chain of businesses that feel they don't have a common ground; promoting the business interest of the members, and fostering growth within the region.
The site would provide a platform for these professionals or businesseses to interact within themselves and also for local residents to be aware of the various business within their areas as well as Events about them. Lastly, the platform would have partnership with the government to also ensure that public projects within the region are done by the local business members/professionals.
