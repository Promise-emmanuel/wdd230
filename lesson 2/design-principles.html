Principles of Design

PARC: Alignment

In design, the principles of alignment refers to the way the different elements in a design are lined up or arranged in relation to each other or to a particular part of the page.
The elements can be centered, right or left-aligned to other items in the design.

PARC: Constrast

Constrast simply means determining what the first thing is that you want the viewer to look at.
It is designing a distinct difference between the foreground and background colors in order to enhance readability.
